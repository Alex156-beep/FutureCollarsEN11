Flower Encyclopedia

Popular Garden Flowers

This website presents basic information about popular garden flowers.

Each flower has a short description and an image.

Flowers are commonly used in gardens, bouquets, and decorations.

Rose

Roses symbolize love and beauty. They are among the most popular flowers in the world.

Tulip

Tulips bloom in spring and come in many bright and beautiful colors.

Sunflower

Sunflowers are tall plants that follow the sun and symbolize happiness.
